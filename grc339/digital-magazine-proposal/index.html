Introduction

On the Crest

"On the Crest" is a digital news publication on the topic of space and launch mission patches. It catalogs various space and launch mission patches and discusses the design aspects, the story behind it, and the deeper meanings of each element within the patch design. Behind every mission patch is a meaning that encapsulates a celebration of a team's work towards a common goal and accomplishment.
